To achieve what you and others think is impossible you have to go insane. You can not get extraordinary results with ordinary thoughts. And if you believe you can't do it then you must become someone who does. To go insane isn't as chaotic as the masses make it seem and it is often the exact thing that you need to achieve the insane.

The reality is that if average people are agreeing with you on a decision then you know it is wrong. Continuing, if you are average and you are agreeing with yourself on a decision you also bet that that decision is wrong as well. You need someone else. You need Tyler Durden. You need to build this character that is not from this world. And you need to go so delusional that you start believing you are them.

The commitment needs to be entire. If you do not commit you will fail. You can not half it, and the worst mistake is thinking you can change yourself, and you can leave a little piece of yourself there. That will be the biggest mistake you can make.

Never try to change yourself, you will find that those insecurities, those past experiences, those voices will always be there. You need to kill your old self and become this new character who knows nothing of those failures, nothing of those insecurities, nothing of those voices. When you become this new character you are everything you were not, without everything you were.
