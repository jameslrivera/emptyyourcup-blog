---
title: "Rule One: Lead by Example"
description: "A good leader leads by example."
category: "on-leadership"
date: 2026-03-10
---

A leader never wants to be seen as a hypocrite for not upholding the values they expect from others. Preaching values that they themselves do not practice. A good leader leads by example, this example instills inspiration in the ranks and confidence in the leader's ability to lead. When you respect the standard others will as well. new book on leadership
